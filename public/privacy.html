United Services Recruiting ("United Services," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website us-unitedservices.com and provides CDL-A driver recruiting services that connect professional truck drivers with hiring carriers across the United States. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you submit an application, contact form, lead form (including forms served through Facebook or Instagram), or otherwise communicate with us, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number, email address, city and state);
  • Professional information relevant to driver placement, such as CDL class, years of driving experience, equipment types you operate, and route preferences;
  • Any additional information you choose to provide in your message or application.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you about driving opportunities that match your preferences;
  • Introduce you to hiring carriers within our partner network;
  • Respond to your questions and provide our recruiting services;
  • Improve our services and communications.

How We Share Your Information

To provide our recruiting service, we may share the professional and contact information you submit with hiring carriers in our network for the purpose of making a job introduction. We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing. We may share information with service providers who help us operate our business (for example, communication and hosting providers), and where required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide our recruiting services and to comply with our legal obligations. You may request that we delete your information at any time using the contact details below.

Your Choices

You may opt out of further communications from us at any time by replying to any message, or by contacting us at the email address below. You may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you.

Consent to Contact

By submitting your information through our forms, you agree that United Services Recruiting may contact you by phone, text message, or email regarding driving opportunities. Message and data rates may apply. You may withdraw this consent at any time.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Platforms

If you reach us through a form on Facebook or Instagram, your submission is also subject to the privacy policy of Meta Platforms, Inc. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party platforms.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for professional drivers and are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

06 / How it works

Three steps to your
next driving job.

From application to first paycheck — here's exactly how we get you on the road.

01
Apply

Apply in 3 minutes.

No résumé. No upload. Just tell us who you are and what you drive — we take it from there.

  • 30s Contact: name, phone, email.
  • 60s CDL class, years driving, equipment experience — multi-select for dry van, flatbed, reefer, step deck, tanker, power only, car hauler.
  • 90s Preferred route (OTR, regional, local, dedicated), SAP status, current location.

SAP-cleared and zero-experience drivers welcome. Save and finish from any device.

02
Match

We match you.

A real recruiter — not an algorithm — pairs your profile with carriers actively hiring right now.

  • Same day A recruiter reviews your profile against 640+ live openings across our 120+ carrier partners.
  • Within 24 hours You get matched offers with pay, lanes, home time and start dates spelled out.
  • Compare side by side Pick 2–4 fits, ask any question — we'll answer with no pressure.

Nothing fits today? We keep your profile warm and call the moment the right offer lands.

03
Drive

Start driving.

You pick the offer. We run the paperwork in the background while you prep for the road.

  • Paperwork DOT application, MVR, drug screen, prior employer verification — handled by us.
  • Orientation Carrier-paid. We coordinate timing, travel and the first dispatch call.
  • First mile Average sign-to-seat is 6 days. First paycheck inside two weeks.

94% of drivers we place are still with the same carrier at 90 days. Lifestyle fit, not just rate.
